skyboxes for the choks engine are stored in an animated webp as follows:
right left top bottom front back

this can be easily done with img2webp, which is a binary distributed with libwebp.

USAGE:
img2webp right left top bottom front back

using https://jaxry.github.io/panorama-to-cubemap/:
img2webp px.png nx.png py.png ny,png pz.png nz.png
